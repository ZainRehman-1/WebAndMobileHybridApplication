BMW X5 Series Overview



The 3rd Generation BMW X5 was introduced in 2013. The 3rd Generation BMW X5 is a four wheel drive front engine mid-size luxury SUV. BMW X5 is available in 1 variant in Pakistan, xDrive35i. While the variants xDrive30d, xDrive40d and M50d are also available in the international market. The BMW X5 3rd Generation featured an updated design language, lighter and stiffer body thanks to the use of more aluminium in its construction. BMW X5 2017 price is competitive in the local market when compared to other models in the category.

Exterior
The exterior of the 3rd Generation BMW X5 sports a unique design language. The front end houses large trapezium styled side swept LED headlights with tube and circular inserts. The classic BMW kidney grille and a trapezium air intake on the large front bumper. The rear end features sleek, angular side swept taillights with tube inserts, dual exhaust tips, and a standard case power tailgate. All variants of the 3rd Generation BMW X5 feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the 3rd Generation BMW X5 features a unique and familiar BMW design language that allows it to differentiate itself from its rivals.

BMW 3 Series Overview



The 6th Generation BMW 3-Series was introduced in 2012. BMW 3-Series 2020 is a front engine rear/four-wheel drive sedan. The BMW 3-Series 6th Generation featured an updated design language compared to the 5th Generation 3-series. BMW 3-Series is one of the best-selling cars of all time and continues to be BMW bestselling model today due its unique looks, drive, and feel. Globally,6th generation 3-Series is available in 9 variants 316d, 318d, 320d, 330d, 335d, 318i, 320i, 328i, 330i and 340i and two body styles sedan and station wagon. In Pakistan only 318i variant is available at the price of 5.41 million.which is fairly competitive when compared to other models in the same class.

Exterior
The exterior of the 6th Generation BMW 3 Series features a unique design language. The front end features sleek side swept headlights with daytime running lights, the iconic BMW kidney grille, a large front bumper with a large trapezium styled air intake that is flanked by standard circular fog lights. The rear end features sharp side swept taillights with a large rear bumper. The station wagon features identical styling to the 4-door sedan apart from being slightly longer to accommodate the rear tailgate. Options include LED Headlights and fog lights, carbon fiber mirror caps, rear spoiler and front splitter, black kidney grille and M colored bumper stripes. The overall exterior of the 6th Generation BMW 3 Series features a fairly unique and distinctive design language that allows it to stand out amongst other cars in its class.
