Audi A3 Overview



The 3rd Generation Audi A3 car was unveiled in 2012. Audi A3 2020 is a front engine front/four wheel drive hatchback. The 3rd Generation Audi A3 features an updated design language and rear multilink suspensions system compared to the previous 2nd Generation Audi A3. The 3rd Generation Audi A3 is based on Volkswagen group MGQ platform. In Pakistan, Audi A3 2020 is available in only one model 1.2 TFSI S Tronic but 1.4 TFSI, 1.6 FSI. 1.8 TFSI, 2.0 FSI, 2.0 TFSI, S3 2.0 TFSI, RS3 2.5 TFSI, 1.6 TDI and 2.0 TDI are also being imported. The 3rd Generation Audi A3 1.2 TFSI is available in 4 variants, 1.2 TFSI Base Model, 1.2 TFSI Standard, 1.2 TFSI Design Line and 1.2 TFSI Exclusive Line.Audi A3 2020 price in Pakistan is between Rs. No Price Set which is competitive when compared to other models in the same class available in the local market.

Exterior
The exterior of the 3rd Generation Audi A3 2020 features a very familiar Audi design language. The front end houses sleek narrow side swept headlights, a large hexagon grille, and circular fog lights. The rear end houses sharp, sleek LED taillights and dual exhaust tips. The 5-door saloon variant features in Audi A3 identical styling at the front but at the rear end, it houses an ordinary rear boot, rectangular styled LED taillights, and dual exhaust tips. The 2-door convertible A3 the same styling as the sedan apart from the power foldable cloth roof. The RS3 features a slightly more aggressive and sporty design language. The front end houses a larger front bumper with dual rectangular styled air intakes with an aluminum accent and Quattro badging. The rear end houses a roof spoiler, dual circular styled sports exhaust tips, and a rear diffuser. Options include a choice of 2 rims, full LED headlights, S line front and rear bumpers and S line fender badges. The overall exterior of the 3rd Generation Audi A3 features a fairly distinctive and familiar Audi design language that allows it to set itself apart from its rivals.
