Suzuki APV Overview



The 1st Generation Suzuki APV (All Purpose Vehicle) was launched in 2005. Suzuki APV 2020 is a front engine front wheel drive MPV. Suzuki APV was introduced to replace the outgoing Suzuki Every. Suzuki APV 2020 was the first MPV to be launched in Pakistan by Pak Suzuki Motors. Suzuki APV 2020 is also referred to as the Suzuki APV Arene and the Mitsubishi Maven in other regions of the world. Suzuki APV 2020 is only available in 1 variant GLX. The Suzuki APV 2020 price in Pakistan is 24 lacs which is not competitive when compared to other cars in the same class available in the local market.

Exterior
The exterior of the Suzuki APV 2020 features a fairly boxy design even for an MPV. The front end houses trapezium side swept headlights, a rectangular grille with dual chrome accents, a large rectangular air intake and standard circular fog lights. The rear end houses rectangular stacked taillights and a standard case tailgate. The overall exterior of the Suzuki APV 2020 features a fairly boxy design that is familiar for a Japanese designed MPV.
