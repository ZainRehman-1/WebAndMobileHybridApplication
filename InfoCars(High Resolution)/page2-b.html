CIAZ

Suzuki Ciaz Overview



Pak Suzuki launched the first generation of Suzuki Ciaz in 2017.The Suzuki Ciaz 2020 is a front engine, front wheel drive subcompact sedan with outstanding features and specifications.The Suzuki Ciaz is an attempt by Suzuki to change the perception in the Pakistan market. Suzuki Ciaz 2020 is offered in two trims in Pakistan, Manual and Automatic. The price of Suzuki Ciaz 2020 is in the range of Rs. 2,300,000 To 2,500,000.

Exterior
The Suzuki Ciaz 2020 exterior features a unique and distinctive design language.The front fascia comprises a fairly large hood and a 4-slat chrome grille with the Suzuki ‘S’ badge in the center.The rear end houses the trapezium styled side swept headlights, a chrome trim piece running the length of the trunk lid and a large rear bumper with aprons. The overall Suzuki Ciaz 2020 exterior stand out amongst its rivals, especially in the local market.

Interior
The Suzuki Ciaz 2020 interior features a unique and distinctive design language.The front fascia comprises a fairly large hood and a 4-slat chrome grille with the Suzuki ‘S’ badge in the center.The rear end houses the trapezium styled side swept headlights, a chrome trim piece running the length of the trunk lid and a large rear bumper with aprons. The overall Suzuki Ciaz 2020 exterior stand out amongst its rivals, especially in the local market.

CULTUS

Suzuki Cultus Overview



Suzuki launched the 3rd generation of Cultus in Pakistan on 22nd April 2017 which is globally recognized as Celerio. Suzuki Cultus 2020 revitalized the whole design framework of Cultus and produced a more sophisticated and bolder cultus. Suzuki Cultus car was first unveiled in 'Auto Expo 2014' in India and later on, it made its way into 'Geneva Motor Show' in 2014. Pak Suzuki launched Cultus (1000cc car) considering the need of fuel-efficient family car and growing trend of imported hatchbacks in the country. Suzuki Cultus 2020 was launched in two variants VXR and VXL(top of the line). The VXL comes with the airbags, power steering, power windows, fog lights and anti-lock braking system while some of the features have been shredded in VXR but it comes at an affable price.

Exterior
Suzuki cultus 2020 has a compact hatchback design. The front facia comprised of angular shaped chrome grill nestled between sleek headlights which are placed above the standard circular shaped fog lights. The rear end houses trapezium shaped upswept stacked tail lights. The overall exterior of Suzuki cultus 2020 is unique and well proportioned and it can easily outrace other imported hatchbacks available in Pakistan.

Interior
Suzuki cultus 2020 has a compact hatchback design. The front facia comprised of angular shaped chrome grill nestled between sleek headlights which are placed above the standard circular shaped fog lights. The rear end houses trapezium shaped upswept stacked tail lights. The overall interior of Suzuki cultus 2020 is unique and well proportioned and it can easily outrace other imported hatchbacks available in Pakistan.

Engine

WAGON R

Suzuki Wagon R Overview



The 1st Generation Suzuki Wagon R was introduced in 2014. Suzuki Wagon R 2020 is a front engine front wheel drive hatchback. Suzuki Wagon R 2020 features the brand new Suzuki K-Series engine that is more economical and quieter than most of the other engines fitted in Pak Suzuki’s range of cars. The 1st Generation Suzuki Wagon R is available in 2 variants Wagon R VXR and Wagon R VXL. Suzuki Wagon R 2020 price in Pakistan ranges between Rs. 1,605,000 To 1,890,000.

Exterior
Suzuki Wagon R 2020 exterior features a familiar Suzuki design language. The front end houses large trapezium styled side swept headlights, a trapezium grille with a chrome accent, a trapezium air intake, and dual standard circular fog lights. The rear end houses a roof spoiler, stacked rectangular vertical side swept taillights, and a standard case hatchback. All variants of the Suzuki Wagon R feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the 1st Generation Suzuki Wagon R 2020 features a fairly restrained design language that is familiar for Japanese hatchback of its type.
