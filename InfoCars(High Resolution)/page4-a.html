Honda BR-V Overview



The Honda BRV was introduced in Pakistan on 21 April 2017, the 7 seater Honda BR-V featured a brand new modern design that was meant to appeal to the Subcompact SUV market. The Price of Honda BR-V 2020 was kept low as compared to the other Subcompact SUV available in the market. Honda BR-V 2020 is offered in 3 variants i-VTEC, i-VTEC MT and i-VTEC S. Standard Features on the i-VTEC and i-VTEC MT include driverside airbag, power windows, infotainment system, rear air vents, digital climate control and retractable side mirrors with indicator lamps. i-VTEC S includes leather seats, rearview camera and alloy wheels as an additional feature. Honda BR-V 2020 price in Pakistan is Rs. 3,079,000 To 3,399,000 which is very competitive when compared to other models in the same class.

Exterior
The Honda BR-V 2020 features active solid motion exterior design. The headlights and front grills are designed to highlight the sporty look. The front facia is comprised of sporty grills nestled between the sleek headlights having project lamps. The rear end also features the use of chrome with a bar running across the centre giving eloquent look to the posterior part Roof rails adds more extravaganza to Honda BRV. The general shape of BR-V is that of a typical subcompact SUV and due to sleek exterior design and unique styling BR-V feels very opulent on the road.

VEZEL

Honda Vezel Overview



The 1st Generation Honda Vezel was introduced in 2013. Honda Vezel 1st Generation is a front engine front/four wheel drive compact SUV. Honda Vezel is also known as the Honda HR-V in other regions of the world. Honda Vezel competes with Toyota C-HR in Pakistani market. The 1st Generation Honda Vezel is based on the same Honda Global Compact Series platform that underpins other cars in Hondas lineups such as the Honda Fit, Honda HR-V and the Honda City. Honda Vezel 2020 price in Pakistan is quite competitive when compared to other models in the local market.

Exterior
The Honda Vezel exterior of the 1st Generation features familiar Honda design language. The front end houses sleek narrow side swept headlights with tube designed LED daytime running light inserts, a small chrome grille, a trapezium air intake and dual fog lights. The rear end houses a roof spoiler with an integrated brake light, sharp side swept taillights, and a standard case tailgate. All variants of the Honda Vezel feature identical styling in every aspect. The overall exterior of the 1st Generation Honda Vezel features a fairly unique but familiar Honda design language that allows it to differentiate itself from other cars in the same class.
