Audi R8 Overview



The 1st Generation Audi R8 was introduced in 2007. The 1st Generation R8 is a mid-engined 2-seater all wheel drive supercar. The Audi R8 1st generation is based upon the same platform that underpins the Lamborghini Gallardo. The Audi R8 is considered by many to be “the best handling road car today” and one of the only truly usable supercars. The 1st Generation R8 was given a facelift in 2012, it received a minor series of cosmetic upgrades and a dual 7-speed dual clutch transmission. The Audi R8 is available in 3 variants R8, R8 V10 and R8 V10 Plus. The 1st Generation Audi R8 lasted In production for over 9 years. The 1st Generation Audi R8 was available to purchase in the years 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 and the year 2017.The 1st Generation Audi R8 2017 price was very competitive when compared to other supercars in the same performance class.

Exterior
The exterior of the 1st Generation Audi R8 features a very distinctive design language but is still instantly recognizable as an Audi. The front fascia features slim narrow side swept headlights with integrated daytime running lights, dual air intakes below the headlights and a large trapezium styled grille. The sides feature large side vents and the R8 signature sideblades. The rear end houses trapezium shaped taillights with a tube design, large dual grilles bellow the headlights and large dual circular sports exhaust tips. The R8 Plus variant features a small fixed lip spoiler on the rear deck and a larger rear diffuser. The facelift variant introduced a series of cosmetic upgrades that included new headlights that incorporated a tubular design, new wheel designs, circular exhaust outlets and new taillights with a tubular design. The overall exterior of the Audi R8 features aggressive styling that is portioned nicely and is able to easily stand out amongst its rivals. Options include a choice between 3 different rim styles, carbon ceramic brakes, carbon sigma sideblades and carbon fiber engine compartment inlays.

Audi Q7 Overview



The second generation of Audi Q7 was introduced in 2016, Audi Q7 2020 featured a brand new more modern design that was meant to appeal the high-end SUV market. The Audi Q7 2020 is available in only one variant in Pakistan, 3.0 TFSI Quattro. However, Audi Q7 is available in 4 variants globally, 2.0 TFSI Quattro, 3.0 TFSI Quattro, 3.0 TDI Quattro, ultra 3.0 TDI Quattro. Audi Pakistan is expected to launch these variants soon in Pakistan.The price of Audi Q7 3.0 TFSI Quattro in Pakistan is 20.5 million.

Exterior
Audi Q7 features a brand new more modern and premium design language. The front facia is comprised of an angular rectangular shaped chrome grille nestled between the sleek side-swept headlights which are placed above the standard circular shaped fog lights. The rear end also features the aesthetically designed side swept taillights with the addition of protracted bumper lights. The overall Audi Q7 2020 exterior stand out amongst its rivals especially in the local market.
